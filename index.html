What is Meanwise?

Meanwise provides an end-to-end solution for people to prove their individual skills - built on Blockchain. Meanwise enables people with diverse skillsets to build a profile where they can validate their abilities with stackable credits.

The three main components of our solution are:

CryptoID

smart.credits

Profile

CryptoID

CryptoID is an open and secure authentication system that uses biometrics to verify your identity. Your biometric information is encrypted and stored in the Blockchain. We donâ€™t store any of the data associated with the CryptoID in our servers.

smart.credits

smart.credits is a dynamic system that validates your skills by using data from verified independent critics who publicly vouch for your work, along with data from educational institutions, employers and other credentials.

Profile

The Meanwise Profile is the digital alternative to the traditional Resume. The Profile combines information from smart.credits and your Portfolio to visualize your abilities in a beautiful design.
